## Pub Illustration
Misfits Pub is a legendary bar for mercenaries and seekers located in the City of Memories. The wall behind the bar is adorned with a variety of posters. The large central poster prominently features Eva Chan, a legendary figure known for her mastery in both technological and mystical aspects of the world. She is a tiny and delicate woman with long green hair and green eyes, subtle arm augmentations, two custom guns on her belt, and advanced gear.
Other posters include advertisements for rock bands, famous persons, upcoming events, mercenary services, mystical artifacts, cybernetic enhancements, and other random posters to create a lively and eclectic atmosphere. The bar has a mix of techno-modern decor and magical artifacts, illustrating the duality of the place. The atmosphere is lively, with mercenaries and seekers enjoying their time, the bartender Alex, an ex-mercenary, standing behind the counter, and the staff, including the sexy waiter Nancy, serving customers.
